The Problem

AI training is hitting a wall on Earth.

Data centers consume 4% of global electricity. Cooling alone devours 40% of that. As models scale to trillions of parameters, the physics of terrestrial compute are breaking down.

🔥

Thermal Bottleneck

GPUs generate extreme heat. Terrestrial data centers spend billions on cooling infrastructure that still can't keep up with next-gen chip densities.

⚡

Energy Crisis

A single large model training run can consume the annual electricity of a small city. Power grids are buckling under AI demand, and costs are skyrocketing.

📍

GPU Scarcity

Wait times for H100 clusters stretch to 6+ months. Hyperscalers hoard capacity. Startups and researchers are priced out of the game entirely.

The Solution

Compute, untethered from Earth.

Our orbital server clusters leverage the vacuum of space for passive cooling, solar power for unlimited energy, and zero-gravity chip architectures for unmatched density.
